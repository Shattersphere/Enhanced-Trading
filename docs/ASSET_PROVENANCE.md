## Rules

- Do not add third-party assets without source and license/permission notes.
- Do not publish private, placeholder, generated, or externally restricted assets without review.
- Keep generated assets reproducible when practical; document the generator and source inputs.
- If provenance is unclear, mark it `TODO[unknown]` and exclude from public release until resolved.
- Do not reintroduce generated badge sprites or patched-core badge assets into this repo.
